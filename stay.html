Airbnb

If you want more flexibility on location and price, Airbnb is a good option. To the unfamiliar, Airbnb is a website where you can book lodging in other people's homes. You can rent a private room or a whole house. Many San Francisco residents post their homes on Airbnb, so there are plenty of choices.

Prices vary
