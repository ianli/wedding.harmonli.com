Things to do

Welcome to SF! Here is a map (or printable PDF) of the few critical places we will be this weekend PLUS our favorite sites off the 49-Mile Scenic Drive. The 49-Mile Scenic Drive is a designated scenic road tour highlighting much of San Francisco, CA.

You may have noticed that the official 49-Mile Scenic Drive sign has a big influence on our wedding theme. We were inspired since these signs are all over Dogpatch, where Ian used to live.

Footwear

The wedding ceremony is in a park, so please consider this in your selection of footwear. If you are wearing heels, consider getting heel protectors. Also, the park is a popular play area for dogs, so keep your eyes peeled when walking.

Weather

In case of rain, we will move the ceremony to Dogpatch Wineworks, which is only 2 blocks away. We will update this webpage if this happens.

If it doesn’t rain, it’s possible that it might be chilly outside, so bring a light coat. Here is a link to San Francisco weather.
